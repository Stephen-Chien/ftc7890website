Our Impact

We greatly value our community, both within the Bronx and in STEM overall. 7890 aims to spread the passion of robotics and computer science in our community using our passion, resources and experiences. 7890 also wants to establish an altruistic presence by supporting causes outside of robotics and inside our community as a team.

Balancing Individual Achievement and a Caring Community
na

Five freshman founded 7890 “Space Lions” six years ago. This year, our team has become the largest it has ever been, with 15 members. What makes 7890 unique is the camaraderie and passion our team has. Through many hours of hard work, we have bonded to become friends, even outside of robotics. We foster a welcoming team dynamic where everyone is free to bring up ideas even if they are not too sure about if it will work. Every team has its ups and downs, but 7890 members always make a point to learn from both our highs and lows. Our team is broken up into 3 divisions: Hardware, Software and Operations which allows everyone on the team to specialize in a division that they have interest in and secondary in another which they feel that they want more experience in. This arrangement makes it so that if fewer people are needed on a certain day in one division, they can work in their secondary division.
